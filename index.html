Data Pipeline

Detection to Alert in Five Stages

Automated end-to-end workflow from satellite scan to actionable early warning.

01

Detect

VIIRS satellite scans identify thermal anomalies. Agricultural masking filters crop burning from industrial sources.

02

Classify

XGBoost classifier distinguishes agricultural from non-agricultural fires using land cover, NDVI, and spatiotemporal features.

03

Transport

Wind trajectory simulation models pollutant corridor transport from Punjab and Haryana toward Delhi over the 72-hour window.

04

Predict

LSTM model forecasts PM2.5 delta at Delhi stations with SHAP-explainable feature attribution and confidence intervals.

05

Alert

Multi-channel threshold-triggered alerts issued 24-72 hours before predicted AQI breach events.
